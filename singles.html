BRIEF - A bunch of artwork I painted during the spring 2023, I was Inspired to bring humor once again, through scenes and moments in my life. I have two mischievouss cats, which were the inspiration for MESSY CREATIVE. In MANGO GIRLS I capture two girlfriends having a chat. l love smoking - it's bad for you (so now I rarley smoke), but how great is the 90's attitude and old movies where everyone smoke and talk. SPA PUPPIES were a lot of fun to create, these comemorate a time in my life as a dog groomer, it was fun, bubbly, wiggly.

MEDIUM - Mixed media (Acrylics, Graphite, Pastels, Spray paints and colored pencils) on 300 g/m Archival Fabriano paper.
