SAFE TO BE WILD

BRIEF - Is it safe to be wild? Does wild mean the same thing for all of us? Does it mean we are overlly aggressive, or maybe not responsible and unhinged?
This series comemorates a joint meaning between indangered animals like the OCELOT or overly common ones like RAVEN and HYENA, with the meaning of the word "Wild" in relation to our true self. I prefer to live with continious search for the wilderness and the way it lives in us and around us. And to let this sneaky, at times indangered and at times overlly common quality in us, be noticed.
